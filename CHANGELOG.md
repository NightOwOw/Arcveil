# ArcVeil Changelog

## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-10

### 🆕 New
- Initial release of ArcVeil
- Relationship map canvas with 6 node types and 9 edge styles
- Deep character profiles: Basic, Appearance, Powers, Voice, Arc, Interview, Chemistry
- Writing Hub with document editor, entity linker, annotations, version history
- Fantasy map generator with Voronoi regions, rivers, and city pins
- Story structure: timeline, scene kanban, arc management, beats checker, pacing
- World & Lore: locations, factions, bestiary, items, world calendar, culture builder
- Companion system: Ciona and Somvora with awareness engine
- Overlay system: floating HUD, edge panel, quick summon, system tray
- 11 built-in themes with full color token editor
- Local-first: everything on your machine, no internet required
